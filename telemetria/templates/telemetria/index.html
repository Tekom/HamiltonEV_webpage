What is HamiltonEV?

Our team, comprised of mechanical engineering students, is responsible for designing a car to participate in the Shell Eco-Marathon. We work on creating a highly energy-efficient vehicle, with the goal of achieving the maximum distance traveled using the least amount of fuel. Our tasks span from engineering and aerodynamics to component optimization, all contributing to the sustainable future of mobility.

Shell Eco-marathon

The Shell Eco-Marathon is a global competition that invites student teams to design, build, and race vehicles aiming to achieve the highest possible energy efficiency. Participants strive to cover the longest distance using the least amount of energy, promoting innovation in eco-friendly transportation solutions and fostering a commitment to sustainable mobility.
